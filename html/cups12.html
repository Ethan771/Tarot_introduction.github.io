Knight of Cups

Symbolism: The Knight of Cups typically shows a knight, often in shining armor, riding a white horse. Unlike the other knights in the Tarot, this knight's horse is usually calm or moving at a gentle pace. The knight holds a cup, suggesting that his quest or mission is related to emotions, love, or the arts. The calm waters and clear skies in the background often symbolize clarity of emotion and peace. The card's imagery suggests a romantic, dreamy, and charming energy, as well as a quest for emotional or artistic expression.

Upright Meaning: When the Knight of Cups appears in a reading in its upright position, it often signifies romantic gestures, charm, and a quest for emotional discovery. This knight is in touch with his emotions and is not afraid to express or act on them.

Reversed Meaning: In its reversed position, the Knight of Cups can indicate moodiness, daydreaming, or disappointments in love.

In a Reading: In a Tarot reading, the Knight of Cups often represents someone who is a dreamer, romantic, and intuitive. This person is in touch with their emotions and is not afraid to express them. The card can also suggest a situation that requires you to act with your heart rather than your head, or it may indicate a forthcoming proposal, invitation, or message.
