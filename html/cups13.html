Queen of Cups

Symbolism: The Queen of Cups is often depicted sitting on a throne by the sea, holding a beautiful, ornate cup. The cup, often closed unlike the other cups in the suit, signifies the depth of her emotions and the mysteries of the subconscious. The calm sea and clear sky around her symbolize emotional tranquility and clarity. The card's imagery exudes a sense of deep emotional understanding, intuition, and compassion.

Upright Meaning: When the Queen of Cups appears in a reading in its upright position, she often signifies emotional maturity, compassion, and a strong intuitive sense. She represents someone who is in touch with their emotions and the emotions of others, able to provide comfort and understanding.

Reversed Meaning: In its reversed position, the Queen of Cups can indicate emotional insecurity, dependency, or moodiness.

In a Reading: In a Tarot reading, the Queen of Cups often represents a person who is emotionally mature, compassionate, and intuitive. She may be a confidante or someone who provides emotional support. The card can also suggest a situation where emotional understanding, intuition, or artistic appreciation is highlighted.
