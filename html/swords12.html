Knight of Swords

Symbolism: The Knight of Swords typically depicts a knight in armor, charging forward on a powerful steed with a sword raised high. The background often shows clouds or a turbulent sky, suggesting a storm or strong winds. This imagery represents swift action, aggression, and ambition.

Upright Meaning: When the Knight of Swords appears in a reading in its upright position, it often signifies swift action, determination, and assertiveness.

Reversed Meaning: In its reversed position, the Knight of Swords can indicate recklessness, impulsiveness, or a lack of strategy.

In a Reading: In a Tarot reading, the Knight of Swords often serves as a symbol of swift and determined action. It suggests that one is charged with energy and ready to pursue their goals with vigor. The card emphasizes the importance of being assertive and direct, but also serves as a reminder to ensure actions are well-thought-out. When reversed, it's a caution against acting too hastily or without a clear plan.
