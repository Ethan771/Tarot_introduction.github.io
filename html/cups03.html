Three of Cups

Symbolism: The Three of Cups typically depicts three individuals, often women, joyfully raising their cups in a toast or celebration. They might be dancing or simply standing close together, symbolizing camaraderie and shared joy. The atmosphere of the card is festive, and there may be fruits or flowers around them, representing abundance and happiness. The card is rich in symbolism related to celebration, friendship, and communal joy.

Upright Meaning: When the Three of Cups appears in a reading in its upright position, it often signifies celebration, friendship, and communal happiness. It can represent a gathering of friends or family, a special occasion, or simply the joy of being in good company.

Reversed Meaning: In its reversed position, the Three of Cups can indicate overindulgence, a falling out among friends, or the need to focus on oneself.

In a Reading: In a Tarot reading, the Three of Cups often serves as a reminder to celebrate life's joys and to cherish the bonds you have with friends and loved ones. Whether you're marking a special occasion, enjoying a gathering, or simply relishing the company of good friends, this card encourages you to embrace the moment and share your joy with others.
