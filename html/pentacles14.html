King of Pentacles

Symbolism: The King of Pentacles typically depicts a mature and regal man sitting on a lavish throne, holding a single pentacle, surrounded by symbols of wealth, abundance, and nature. This imagery represents mastery over the material world, wealth, leadership, and stability.

Upright Meaning: When the King of Pentacles appears in a reading in its upright position, it often signifies wealth, leadership, and a solid foundation.

Reversed Meaning: In its reversed position, the King of Pentacles can indicate greed, stubbornness, or being overly materialistic.

In a Reading: In a Tarot reading, the King of Pentacles often serves as a reminder of the virtues of leadership, stability, and financial acumen. It suggests that with a grounded and practical approach, one can achieve great success in the material world. The card also emphasizes the importance of using one's wealth and influence wisely and generously, ensuring that success benefits not just oneself but also the broader community.
