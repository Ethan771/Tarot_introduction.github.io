King of Cups

Symbolism: The King of Cups is often depicted sitting on a throne, either on a ship amidst the turbulent sea or on a stone platform by the water. Despite the turbulent waters around him, he appears calm and composed, symbolizing his mastery over his emotions. He holds a cup in one hand and a scepter in the other, representing his emotional depth and control. The card's imagery conveys a sense of emotional balance, wisdom, and diplomacy.

Upright Meaning: When the King of Cups appears in a reading in its upright position, he often signifies emotional balance, compassion, and diplomatic leadership. He represents someone who is in control of their emotions, able to provide guidance and support to others.

Reversed Meaning: In its reversed position, the King of Cups can indicate emotional manipulation, moodiness, or avoidance.

In a Reading: In a Tarot reading, the King of Cups often represents a person who is emotionally mature, wise, and diplomatic. This individual provides guidance, support, and wise counsel, especially in emotional matters. The card can also suggest a situation where emotional balance, diplomacy, or artistic appreciation is highlighted.
