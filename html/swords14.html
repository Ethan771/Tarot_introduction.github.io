King of Swords

Symbolism: The King of Swords typically depicts a mature man sitting on a throne, holding an upright sword. He often appears stern or authoritative, with a clear sky in the background, symbolizing clarity of thought. The landscape might be mountainous, suggesting lofty thoughts or ideals. This imagery represents authority, clear judgment, and intellectual power.

Upright Meaning: When the King of Swords appears in a reading in its upright position, it often signifies authority, clarity, and intellectual leadership.

Reversed Meaning: In its reversed position, the King of Swords can indicate manipulation, being overly authoritative, or detached.

In a Reading: In a Tarot reading, the King of Swords often represents someone who leads with their intellect and values clarity and truth. This person is often in a position of authority and is respected for their wisdom and fair judgment. The card suggests approaching situations with a clear mind, valuing truth, and leading with integrity. When reversed, it serves as a caution against misusing power or being overly detached in decisions.
