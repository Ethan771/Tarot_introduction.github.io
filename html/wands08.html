Eight of Wands

Symbolism: The Eight of Wands typically depicts eight wands or staffs flying through the air, moving swiftly in the same direction. The background might show a clear sky, open landscape, or flowing river, suggesting rapid movement and clear passage. This imagery represents speed, rapid development, and swift communication.

Upright Meaning: When the Eight of Wands appears in a reading in its upright position, it often signifies rapid movement, swift changes, and communication.

Reversed Meaning: In its reversed position, the Eight of Wands can indicate delays, miscommunication, or feeling stuck.

In a Reading: In a Tarot reading, the Eight of Wands often serves as a symbol of rapid movement and swift changes. It suggests that things are progressing quickly, and there might be a flurry of activity or communication. The card emphasizes the importance of being prepared for swift developments and being adaptable. When reversed, it serves as a reminder to be patient, ensure clear communication, and understand that sometimes delays can occur.
