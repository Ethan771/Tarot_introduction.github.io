Temperance (Card XIV)

Symbolism: The Temperance card usually depicts an angelic figure standing with one foot on land and the other in water, symbolizing the balance between the material and spiritual worlds. The figure often holds two cups or jugs and is pouring liquid from one to the other, representing the flow of life and the balance between opposites. A path may lead to a mountain in the distance, symbolizing a spiritual journey or quest for enlightenment.

Upright Meaning: When the Temperance card appears in a reading in its upright position, it often signifies balance, moderation, and harmony. The card encourages you to find a middle ground in your relationships, work, or personal endeavors. It's a card of healing and peace, urging you to cultivate balance in all aspects of your life.

Reversed Meaning: In its reversed position, the Temperance card can indicate imbalance, excess, or a lack of harmony. It suggests that you may be going to extremes in some area of your life, or that you're struggling to find a sense of balance.

In a Reading: In a Tarot reading, the Temperance card serves as a reminder to strive for balance, moderation, and harmony in all aspects of your life. Whether you're dealing with a challenging relationship, a demanding job, or a personal struggle, this card encourages you to take a step back and assess how you can create a more balanced and harmonious situation. It's a card that often appears when you're in need of healing or are on a journey toward spiritual growth.
