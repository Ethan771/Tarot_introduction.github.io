King of Wands

Symbolism: The King of Wands typically depicts a mature man sitting on a throne, holding a wand or staff. He might be surrounded by symbols of lions, salamanders, or other fiery creatures, suggesting leadership, vision, and dynamism. This imagery represents authority, vision, and charisma.

Upright Meaning: When the King of Wands appears in a reading in its upright position, it often signifies leadership, vision, and determination.

Reversed Meaning: In its reversed position, the King of Wands can indicate impulsiveness, arrogance, or a lack of direction.

In a Reading: In a Tarot reading, the King of Wands often serves as a symbol of leadership and vision. He represents someone who is both dynamic and wise, able to lead with passion while also considering the bigger picture. The card emphasizes the importance of having a clear direction and using one's authority responsibly. When reversed, it's a caution against acting too hastily or letting power go to one's head.
