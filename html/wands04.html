Four of Wands

Symbolism: The Four of Wands typically depicts four wands standing upright, forming a structure similar to a canopy or a celebratory gateway. Beneath or between the wands, there might be garlands or flowers, suggesting a festive atmosphere. In the background, there are often figures celebrating or dancing, and a secure or prosperous-looking home or castle might be visible. This imagery represents celebration, harmony, and stability.

Upright Meaning: When the Four of Wands appears in a reading in its upright position, it often signifies celebration, joy, and stability.

Reversed Meaning: In its reversed position, the Four of Wands can indicate conflicts, instability, or a lack of celebration.

In a Reading: In a Tarot reading, the Four of Wands often serves as a symbol of joyous occasions and stability. It suggests that it's a time for celebration, whether marking a special event or simply enjoying the harmony and stability in one's life. The card emphasizes the importance of recognizing and celebrating achievements, big or small. When reversed, it's a reminder to address any underlying issues that might be disrupting harmony or stability.
