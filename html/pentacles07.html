Seven of Pentacles

Symbolism: The Seven of Pentacles typically portrays a man leaning on a staff, looking contemplatively at a bush or tree bearing pentacle fruits. This imagery suggests themes of patience, reflection, and the results of hard work and effort over time.

Upright Meaning: When the Seven of Pentacles appears in a reading in its upright position, it often signifies assessment, patience, and the anticipation of rewards after hard work.

Reversed Meaning: In its reversed position, the Seven of Pentacles can indicate impatience, lack of reward, or re-evaluation of efforts.

In a Reading: In a Tarot reading, the Seven of Pentacles often serves as a reminder of the importance of patience and reflection. It suggests that while immediate results might not be visible, the efforts invested will bear fruit in time. The card also emphasizes the value of taking a moment to assess one's progress and consider whether the current path aligns with long-term goals.
